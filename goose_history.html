The Clash of quack

The clash of quack was the beggining of the end for the Goose empire. As the conflict between the ducks and geese grew stronger the goose empire found themselfs cornered with the duckish armada advancing in the south and western barbarian hoard diserupting their supply of Erbium.

At the end the Goose empire found themselfs defeated when the ducks in alliance with the human race and were able to breach the eastern sheild walls and advance into the Goose empire. When the ducks' northen legion had seiged control of Mound Goose the battle was over and the geese militia went into involuntary exil.
